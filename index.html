About the initiative

What is the Alumni TimeBank?

A platform connecting IITB alumni willing to donate time with students and faculty who need mentoring, guidance, or problem-solving. Matching is curated to make every session meaningful.

For alumni contributors

  • Flexible time commitment
  • Mentor across disciplines
  • Remote or in-person
  • Volunteer-driven, no fees

For students & faculty

  • Expert guidance on demand
  • Access to alumni insights
  • Fast response time
  • Zero-cost support

FAQ

Common questions

Is there any fee involved?

No. This is a volunteer-driven initiative with zero fees.

What is the minimum time commitment?

Donors decide their availability; even a single hour makes a difference.

Can sessions be remote?

Yes, sessions can be remote or in person depending on donor preference.

Who runs this initiative?

The IITB Alumni Association and volunteer alumni coordinators.
